  10. Define a procedure every? which takes two formal parameters:
    • pred is a predicate, and
    • seq (i.e., "sequence") is a list.
    • (every? pred seq) evaluates to #t when every element of seq satisfies pred, and evaluates to #f otherwise.

    • What should happen if the list is empty? Justify your answer in a (clearly written) comment. Here's a hint: if seq1 and seq2 are lists, then it should be the case that

      should be the same as (and (every? pred seq1) (every? pred seq2))

    • Please note: I am asking you for what should happen. So in particular, if you write something like "I tried it out and this is what happened.", your answer is automatically incorrect.
